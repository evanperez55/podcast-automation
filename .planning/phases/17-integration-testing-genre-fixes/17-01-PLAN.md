<objective>
Make clip selection and compliance checking genre-aware so non-comedy podcasts get appropriate behavior.

Purpose: The clip criteria prompt is hardcoded for comedy ("Funny or entertaining moments", "fake problems discussions") and the compliance prompt always tells GPT-4o this is a comedy podcast. Both must be conditional on the client's genre configuration. Additionally, RMS energy candidates should be suppressible for interview/true-crime genres where audio energy is flat and misleading.

Output: Updated content_editor.py, content_compliance_checker.py, client_config.py with genre-aware behavior, plus comprehensive tests.
</objective>
